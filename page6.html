The Girls' Diary Project: Writing Ourselves Into Being

This book reports on a five-year long participatory research project that studied girls' self-presentation and self-awareness in adolescence. The book invites you to learn about the inner lives of girls, to begin to honour and understand the intense and complex passage into adulthood as it is expressed by girls themselves.
