Synchro Boy Teacher Guide available!

Before I was an author, I was a BC certified secondary English teacher—and as in Instructional Designer, I've drafted learning resources for K-12 teachers. Now I've decided to make a teacher guide for Synchro Boy. It's a work-in-progress. Feel free to download and use what's available so far. I'll upload updated versions as I finish them.

This draft of the Synchro Boy Teacher Guide includes an overview section with character list, setting description, and plot summary, a page of whole-book discussion questions, and two pages of book project ideas. It's available as a Word document, so that you can select what you wish to use and change as needed.  

Coming Soon: Chapter discussion questions, quizzes, more whole-book dicussion questions, proficiency scales for assessment of book projects, and descriptions of how the guide aligns to the BC and Ontario curriculum, and Common Core Standards in the US.
